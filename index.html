  • Who?

    The Question Club was organized primarily by Ryan Hoover and Nathan Bashaw

  • What?

    It's simple, really. We pose a question to a respected group of bloggers, and post their answers here.

  • When?

    We're not sure yet. It depends on how well this goes. Probably once a month-ish.

  • Where?

    The bloggers posts live on their own blogs, we just curate and link to them.

  • Why?

    We wanted blogging to be more fun, and to have more of a community feel.
